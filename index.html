The 2026 Tax Reform: The "Full Gist" for Every Nigerian

President Tinubu don sign four new laws wey go change how we handle money from January 1, 2026. No be just grammar; e get how e take affect your pocket. Follow me, make I break am down.

1. The Four "Power Rangers" of the New Law

Government no just pack everything into one book. They share am into four different "Acts" wey go work together:

  • The Nigeria Tax Act (NTA): This one na the "Baba" of the laws. E tell us wetin we go pay, who go pay, and how much exactly.
  • The Nigeria Tax Administration Act (NTAA): This one focus on the "How." How you go file your tax online, how they go track your money, and how you go pay without stress.
  • The Nigeria Revenue Service Act (NRSA): FIRS don change name! Say hello to the NRS. They get more power now to track every kobo wey dey move for the federation.
  • The Joint Revenue Board Act (JRBA): This one na to stop "Double Taxation." No be say Federal go collect, State go collect, and Local Government go still come your shop. They go dey work together now.

2. The ‚Ç¶800,000 Shield (Low Income Protection)

If your total salary or income in a year no reach ‚Ç¶800,000, the law say you be "Tax Exempt." That means your tax na exactly ‚Ç¶0.00. This na big win for minimum wage earners and small hustlers because before, even ‚Ç¶300k earners dey pay small tax. Now, that money stay for your pocket for bread and milk.

3. Rent & Future Relief (Paying Less Legally)

The law now sabi say life hard. So they give you "Consolidated Reliefs":

  • Rent Relief: You fit remove 20% of your house rent (up to ‚Ç¶500k) from your taxable income.
  • Pension & Health: Anything wey you dey pay for Pension, NHIS, or Housing Fund (NHF), they go remove am before they calculate your tax. The more you save for your future, the less tax you pay today.

4. Small Business "Peace of Mind"

If you get small business (hustle) wey the total "turnover" (money wey enter) no reach ‚Ç¶50 Million in a year, you no go pay Company Income Tax (CIT) again. Government want make you use that money grow your business, buy more goods, or hire more people.

5. Why Bank "Narrations" Matter Now

NRS (formerly FIRS) get new digital eyes. If they see ‚Ç¶100k enter your account, the system fit think say na business profit. But if na your brother send you money for Mama hospital bill, use our "Gift / Family Support" tag. This one go tell the system say: "Abeg shift, this one no be profit, na family matter!"

6. What to Expect (The Digital Shift)

From 2026, everything go dey digital. You no need to go carry paper go tax office. Everything go happen for your phone or computer. But wait o, because everything dey digital, they go fit see person wey dey hide money fast fast. Compliance na the new "cool."

7. Who Collects What? (Federal vs. State vs. Local)

Small business owners often face "Double Taxation" because they don't know which agency is legally allowed to collect specific fees. Per the 2026 Guidelines, here is the official breakdown:

NRS (Federal)

  • CIT: Company Income Tax (for big companies).
  • VAT: Value Added Tax (7.5% on goods/services).
  • Education Tax: For registered companies.
  • Stamp Duties: On electronic transfers and docs.

State Government

  • PAYE: Personal Income Tax from your salary.
  • Withholding Tax: On individuals/contracts.
  • Road Taxes: Vehicle license and plate numbers.
  • Business Premises: Yearly registration of your shop.

Local Government

  • Shop & Kiosk Rates: For small containers/stalls.
  • Signage & Advertisement: For your shop boards.
  • Liquor Licenses: If you sell drinks.
  • Radio/TV License: For shop electronics.

The 2026 reform na to make tax "progressive." E mean say: if you earn small, you pay zero or very small. If you be "Big Man" wey dey earn millions, you go pay your fair share to help build the country.
